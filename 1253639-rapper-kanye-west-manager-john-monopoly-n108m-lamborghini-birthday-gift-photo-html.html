Rapper Kanye West gives manager John Monopoly N108m Lamborghini as birthday gift (photo)

- Popular rapper Kanye West has surprised his manager with a customised Lamborghini on his birthday

- The rapper showed up with the car to his manager's birthday ceremony at an eatery West Hollywood, California

- West and his manager, John Monopoly, have known each other for 30 years

American rapper Kanye West known for doing things over the top has done it again. The rapper recently surprised his talent manager, John Monopoly, with a brand new Lamborghini SUV on his birthday.

The 42-year-old rapper was said to have shown up to his manager's birthday ceremony with the brand new car, which he customised for Monopoly.

According to DailyMail, the rapper surprised the manager with the car at the event, which took place at an eatery in West Hollywood, California, on Wednesday, August 7.
