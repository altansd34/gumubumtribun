Meet Marybeth Byrd, Former 'The Voice' Star, Current Rising Star on 'American Idol'

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing American Idol

ABC/Eric McCandless

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Marybeth Byrd has captured the hearts of audiences as a standout contestant in Season 21 of American Idol. With her exceptional country vocals and captivating performances, she has secured a spot in the show’s top 12. Let’s delve into the life and journey of Marybeth, exploring her experience on The Voice, her role as a country radio host, and her aspirations in the music industry.

Who is Marybeth Byrd?

Marybeth is a 22-year-old singer, who possesses a wealth of experience in the realm of singing competitions. Her impressive talent first garnered attention when she competed on Season 17 of The Voice. During her blind audition, all four coaches turned their chairs, and she ultimately joined Team Legend. Although she didn’t win The Voice, her journey on the show provided her with invaluable exposure and a platform to showcase her soulful voice.

Beyond her accomplishments on reality TV, Marybeth works as a country radio host for Mix 106.3 in Jonesboro (KJBX). As a radio host, she connects with fans and introduces them to new artists and songs while building her own career in the industry.

Marybeth’s journey has not been without obstacles. She has triumphed over a stutter that plagued her throughout her childhood. Despite enduring teasing and undergoing speech therapy, she discovered that her stutter vanished when she sang. Music became her refuge, allowing her to express herself freely and become known as a soulful singer in her small country town.

As Marybeth continues her journey on American Idol, she dreams of carving out a successful career in the music industry. Her passionate performances and distinctive country sound have already won her a dedicated following. Marybeth’s talent, combined with her determination and authenticity, positions her as a rising star with immense potential.

Marybeth Byrd Covers Jason Isbell After Earning Top 12 Slot

Following the announcement of her advancement in the competition, Marybeth took the stage with confidence to deliver her rendition of Jason Isbell’s “Cover Me Up.” The performance was set against a backdrop reminiscent of a dreamy meadow, creating a visually captivating atmosphere. Marybeth’s vocals were delicate yet filled with strength, showcasing her impressive range and ability to convey raw emotion.

Her performance resonated deeply with the judges, Katy Perry, Luke Bryan, and Lionel Richie, all of whom rose to their feet, offering a well-deserved standing ovation. The judges’ expressions reflected their satisfaction and appreciation for the remarkable talent they had just witnessed.

Although the judges did not provide direct feedback, their standing ovation spoke volumes about the impact and quality of Marybeth’s performance. As Marybeth progresses in American Idol‘s top 12, her remarkable talent and captivating performances continue to captivate viewers, cementing her place as an artist to watch.
