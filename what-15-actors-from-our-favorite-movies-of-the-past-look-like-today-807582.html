What 15+ Actors From Our Favorite Movies of the Past Look Like Today

When we rewatch old films, we don’t realize the that actors we see there don’t look like that anymore. We see some of them in new movies and don’t even realize it’s them, just older.

Bright Side decided to check and see what the actors that appeared in the iconic films of our youth look like today.

1. Denise Richards & Casper Van Dien — the brave pilot and soldier from Starship Troopers (1997)

2. Patricia Velasquez — the amazing Anck Su Namun from The Mummy (1999)

3. Amanda Plummer — Yolanda, who played the robber’s girlfriend in Pulp Fiction (1994)

4. Til Schweiger & Jan Josef Liefers — Martin & Rudi who loved life so much in Knockin’ On Heaven’s Door (1997)

5. Matt Doran — Mouse from The Matrix (1999)

6. Edward Furlong — Danny, the younger brother of the main character from American History X (1998)

7. Billy Zane — Rosa’s fiancé from Titanic (1997)

8. Laura Linney — Truman’s wife from The Truman Show (1998)

9. Robin Wright — Jenny, the friend of Forrest Gump (1994)

10. Thora Birch — the daughter of Lester from American Beauty (1999)

11. Tommy Lee Jones — Agent K from the iconic Men in Black (1997)

12. Claire Forlani — the love of Death from Meet Joe Black (1998)

13. Laura San Giacomo — Kit De Luca, Vivian’s friend in Pretty Woman (1990)

14. Bonnie Hunt — the Sarah that caused Alan Parrish to go into the jungle for 26 years in Jumanji (1995)

15. Juliette Lewis — the daughter of the pastor from the iconic From Dusk Till Dawn (1996)

What old films are you ready to watch over and over again? Have the people from our compilation changed a lot over the years?
