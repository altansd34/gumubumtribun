Actor Lege Miamii Leaves Country Ahead of Nationwide Protest: You Don Run Away Ni?

  • Nollywood actor Lege Miamii has travelled out of Nigeria as citizens plan to hit the streets for a nationwide protest

Nigerian actor Kehinde Adams, aka Lege Miamii, has travelled out of Nigeria a day before the planned End Bad Governance protest.
