Super Eagles Legend JJ Okocha Dances, Shows Football Moves As He Features in Flavours Agba Bal

  • Flavour Ijele has shared another clip for the visual of his hit song Agba Baller featuring Super Eagles legend JJ Okocha
  • In the heartwarming clip, Okocha, who was spotted with Flavour, showed off his football skills and dance moves
  • The video has stirred excitement among Nigerians as it comes hours before the Nigerian Super Eagles' match against South Africa at the ongoing AFCON

Afro-highlife singer Chinedu Izuchukwu Okoli, popularly known as Flavour N'abania, has stirred another round of emotions online with a snippet of his new music video for Agba Baller.

Legit.ng recalls reporting that Flavour dropped a teaser featuring skit maker NastyBlaq and nightlife king Cubana Chiefpriest.

Flavour features JJ Okocha in Agba Baller video

In the latest video he shared on his social media timeline on Tuesday, February 6, Flavour was spotted with Nigeria's football legend Jay Jay Okocha.
