How Much Money Does Stephen Merchant Make? Latest Income Salary

Stephen James Merchant (born 24 November 1974) is an English writer, director, radio presenter, comedian, and actor.

Merchant is best known for his collaborations with Ricky Gervais and Karl Pilkington, as the co-writer and co-director of the popular British sitcom The Office (2001-2003), co-writer and co-star of Extras (2005-2007) and co-host of The Ricky Gervais Show in its radio, podcast, audiobook and television formats; the radio version won a bronze Sony Award.

Merchant appeared as himself in the BBC series Life's Too Short (2011-2013), which he co-wrote and co-directed. He also voiced the computer program ConRad on The Simpsons in the 2016 episode "The Girl Code", co-developed the Sky1 travel series An Idiot Abroad (2010-2011) and performs as a stand-up comedian.

Merchant had his first dramatic role in a film when he was cast as Caliban in the superhero film Logan (2017).
