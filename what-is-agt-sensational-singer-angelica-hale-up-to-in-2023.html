What is 'AGT' Sensational Singer, Angelica Hale Up To In 2023?

The young and incredibly talented Filipino-American singer, Angelica Hale has continued to captivate hearts with her sensational vocals and unwavering dedication to her craft. Since her remarkable journey on Season 12 of America’s Got Talent, she has grown into a multifaceted artist, a source of inspiration, and a beacon of positivity.

What is Angelica Hale Up To In 2023?

Hale made her first splash on the global stage during her appearance on AGT, where she wowed audiences and judges with her performances. Despite finishing as the runner-up, she emerged as a true winner, leaving a mark on the show’s history. Born on July 31, 2007, Hale celebrated her 16th birthday with a joyous sweet 16 party surrounded by family and friends this year.

Following her AGT journey, Hale’s career trajectory has been nothing short of remarkable. She released her debut single, “Feel the Magic,” in 2019, solidifying her status as a rising star in the music industry.

Hale’s talents have extended beyond the music scene. She has also ventured into the world of acting, making her presence felt in films like Maya Unstoppable and American Reject. These ventures have showcased her versatility and promise as an entertainer across various mediums.

Hale’s journey goes beyond her artistic pursuits; she has demonstrated a deep commitment to making a positive impact in the world. She is known for her involvement in various charity events and her dedication to using her platform for good. Her philanthropic efforts underscore her compassion and desire to give back to the community.

Angelica Hale Is a Social Media Phenomenon

In the age of digital connectivity, Hale has used social media as a means to connect with her fans and share her life’s journey. With an impressive Instagram following of almost 1.1 million followers, she regularly provides glimpses into her personal and professional life, allowing fans to feel more connected to her journey.

She has also found success on YouTube. Her channel boasts more than 3.18 million subscribers, a testament to her ability to connect with a global audience beyond traditional media. On her channel, she shares a variety of content, including music covers, behind-the-scenes glimpses of her life, and collaborations with fellow artists.

Her income primarily comes from singing in shows, live concerts, her thriving YouTube channel, and her substantial online presence. Hale’s annual salary reportedly exceeds $600,000, reflecting her continuing success and growing influence in the entertainment industry.

Most recent of all, Hale has recently signed on to a brand new, Broadway bound musical. Hale is playing Trisha in the musical, Boop The Musical. The musical opens at the CIBC Theatre in Chicago on November 19, 2023.
