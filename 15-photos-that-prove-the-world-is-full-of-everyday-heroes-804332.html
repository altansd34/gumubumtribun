15 Photos That Prove the World Is Full of Everyday Heroes

When we think of a hero, we often think big, like the Avengers. But you can be a hero to anyone and anytime with just a small kind deed or gesture. Like Keanu Reeves, who has all the patience in the world for his fans. And to top it, he gifted all the stuntmen of John Wick 4 a Rolex.

Bright Side salutes these heroes, for deeds both small and big. Their kindness and big hearts show us the way to do good.

1. “When this old woman was stuck in her car in flood water, this guy rushed over and carried her to safety. HERO!!”

2. “This post office has a helpful size guide if you want to buy a box.”

3. “My wife lost her ID a week ago. This showed up today in the mail, no note. Just a true human act of kindness.”

4. “My new home town has a miniature town to help kids learn how to get around town.”

5. “Gf’s a school teacher for blind children. To help them understand and write words she has Lego-like Braille toys!”

6. Not all heroes wear capes, some just help you silence gas pump ads.

7. “This stray cat walked up to my son and saved him from depression. We adopted him.”

8. “At the library in Peabody, MA. Libraries truly are a treasure.”

9. “I’ve been sneaking around the hospital at night and leaving them in high traffic areas. Using stash yarn.”

10. “A neighbour put out a doggy station with treats and water. To help dogs cool down during the heat wave we are having.”

11. Inclusive band-aids, for all skin tones.

12. “Waiting at the eye doctor and a 95-year-old lady is giving everyone homemade pot holders because it makes her happy.”

13. “Motel 6 has the phone number to Help Line on their soaps.”

14. James Harrison, whose plasma donations have saved over two-and-a-half million babies in the world.

15. Keanu Reeves gifted his stuntmen of John Wick 4, a Rolex watch as a thank you gift.

Have you encountered such real-life heroes, or are you a hero yourself? Share the kindest stories you have experienced in your life with us.
