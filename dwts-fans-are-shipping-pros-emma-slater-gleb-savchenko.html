Dancing with the Stars fans have started shipping pro dancers Emma Slater and Gleb Savchenko. The pair recently posed together for a few photos during Slater’s visit to Las Vegas to support Savchenko’s run with Chippendales.

DWTS Fans Ship Emma Slater, Gleb Savchenko

Savchenko has recently been performing in the Chippendales show in Las Vegas, and some of his DWTS colleagues have visited him. That includes Emma Slater, who posted a series of photos with Savchenko on Instagram earlier this week.

In the pictures, Savchenko and Slater pose by the pool in bathing suits and sunglasses, putting their arms around each other and smiling. As Slater captioned the post, “One of us is a Chippendale. The other one likes chips occasionally. It’s just a beautiful friendship.”

Fans in the comments had a lot to say about the pair, with one person writing, “Anyone else been kinda low key shipping Emma and Gleb?” Another wrote, “I did not recognize him with the glasses and thought oooh Emma got some arm candy.”

Someone else told the dancers to “get married already,” adding that they’re “so perfect together.” Yet another commenter wrote that they thought Slater’s post was “a hard launch” before they saw the word “friendship” in the caption.

Fans Have Shipped Slater with Several Men

Savchenko is far from the first man fans have shipped with Slater since they learned of her split from husband and fellow DWTS pro Sasha Farber. People have speculated that Slater could be dating a few of her partners and co-stars.

In 2022, some fans hoped that Slater might get together with her celebrity partner Trevor Donovan. DWTS host Alfonso Ribeiro even commented on the pair’s chemistry after they performed together. Then last year, rumors swirled that Slater could be dating her Season 32 partner Mauricio Umansky. They denied the reports.

Then late last year, fans speculated that there could be a romance between Slater and fellow pro dancer Alan Bersten. Some even believed that the two might have gotten together while Slater was still with Farber.
