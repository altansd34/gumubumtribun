Wizkid Sells Out Madison Square Garden in One Month, Receives a Plaque

  • Nigerian music star Wizkid has done it again as he put the country on the world map after he sold out the Madison Square Garden in New York
  • A video which has gone viral showed the moment Star Boy received a plaque for his latest feat as he did so in one month
  • Many Nigerians, as well as the singer’s foreign fans, have taken to social media to congratulate him

Nigerian international artist Ayo Balogun, better known as Wizkid, has set a new record on the international scene as he sold out the Madison Square Garden (MGS) in New York in one month.

This comes after Wizkid had his show at the garden on Wednesday, November 16, and he completely sold out the 20,000-capacity hall.

In a video that has gone viral, a top official in charge of the MSG was seen congratulating Wizkid over his new feat and he was presented with a plaque.
