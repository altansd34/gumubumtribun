America’s Got Talent Season 19 kicked off the live shows this week, as 11 acts performed for a chance to move on to the Semifinals. Voting was open Tuesday night, and the results were announced on Wednesday. Three acts were chosen by America to move on.

Which AGT Acts Are Moving Forward This Week?

On Wednesday night, Terry Crews announced the three acts who earned enough votes from viewers at home to move on to the Semifinals. One of those acts is Richard Goodall, the singer who earned Heidi Klum’s Golden Buzzer in the auditions.

Also moving on to the Semifinals are country trio Ashes & Arrows, as well as dog act Roni & Rhythm. That means we had to say goodbye to Golden Buzzer singer Liv Warfield, rap duo Flewnt & Inkabee, baton twirler Phillip Lewis, contortionist Arshiya, and dance groups Attraction Juniors, Hypers Kids Africa, and Los Osos High School.

Meanwhile, Tuesday’s episode featured the first-ever Golden Buzzer during the live shows. Sofia Vergara gave the honor to Hakuna Matata Acrobats, allowing them to skip the Semifinals and sending them straight to the Season 19 Finals.

Adrian Stoica & Hurricane Returned to the Stage

Wednesday night’s results show also featured a performance from Adrian Stoica & Hurricane, the dog act who won Season 18. They performed a funny routine set to “Spice Up Your Life” by the Spice Girls, which had the audience and judges laughing.

After the performance, Stoica shared that it was actually Hurricane’s birthday. When host Terry Crews asked how old she is, Stoica joked that you “never ask a woman” her age. Stoica also shared that Hurricane now has a sister named Diva.

AGT will be back next Tuesday night for another live show, as 11 more acts take the stage for a chance to move on to the Semifinals. The episode will feature yet another Golden Buzzer from one of the judges, sending an act straight to the Finals.
