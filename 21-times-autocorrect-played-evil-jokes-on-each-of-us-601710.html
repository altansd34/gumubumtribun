21 Times Autocorrect Played Evil Jokes on Each of Us

Autocorrect is one of those things that we either hate and don’t use or love and can’t live without. Many people are worried though that using this tool too much will make our relationship with language worse. Also, in many cases, people send awkward messages they definitely had no intention of sending. So maybe next time you use autocorrect, you should double-check the final message before pressing “send.”

Here at Bright Side, we’ve collected some of the funniest autocorrect fails on the internet that will definitely put a smile on your face.
