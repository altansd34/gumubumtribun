Britain’s Got Talent star Loren Allred, who is currently competing on AGT: Fantasy League, has released a new “Anniversary Edition” of her iconic Greatest Showman song, “Never Enough.” Loren was the voice behind the song in the 2017 film.

Loren Allred Releases Anniversary Edition of ‘Never Enough’

During this week’s episode of Fantasy League, Allred took the stage to perform a new arrangement of the song, once again showing off her incredible vocal range. The performance earned her enough votes to move on to the Semifinals.

Now fans can enjoy this new studio version of the song, which takes a more subdued approach at the start before leading into Allred’s soaring vocals. The performance ends with a sustained high note that is sure to give listeners chills.

This new edition of the song features a cover image that depicts Allred covered in pearl jewelry, including necklaces, earrings, and bracelets. She even has pearl-decorated nails to complete the look. The singer crosses her hands over her chest in the striking image.

Allred Is Competing on AGT: Fantasy League

Allred is one of 40 acts who were chosen to return to the Got Talent stage for AGT: Fantasy League. The singer, who reached the Finals on Britain’s Got Talent in 2022, is competing on judge Simon Cowell’s dream team.

During Monday night’s new episode, Allred took the stage to sing “Never Enough,” earning a standing ovation from the judges. Cowell said the performance was “honestly spectacular,” adding that Allred is an “extraordinarily good artist.”

Thanks to the audience votes, Allred will now move on to the AGT: Fantasy League Semifinals. Are you rooting for her to win the show? What do you think of this new edition of “Never Enough”? Let us know in the comments below.
