Meet 'AGT's Finger Dance Master Oswaldo Colina

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Over the years, fans have witnessed multiple puppet performances on America’s Got Talent. Oswaldo Colina is nonetheless still a unique variety act, using his talented fingers to create a sublime choreography.

Oswaldo Colina was in Britain’s Got Talent

Hailing from Venezuela, Colina had always dreamt of becoming famous. Thus, while he was already an engineer at 40, he tried to shoot his shot on Britain’s Got Talent.

During his audition, he impressed everyone with his peculiar puppet-like routine. Basically, Colina decorates his fingers and dresses the same as lower legs. Consequently, she successfully managed to blow the judging panel away with his performance.

“We’ve never seen it before and there’s a reason why,” Simon Cowell commented.

While he was a pleasant force in the competition, Colina didn’t make it to the Semi-Finals. Apparently, he failed to sway Cowell ahead of the said round. In addition, Oswaldo took the Spain’s Got Talent stage in the same year. Although Cowell didn’t like Oswaldo’s talent on BGT, the judges on SGT were quickly obsessed.

On Instagram and TikTok, Colina is generous enough to give his followers a free show. For months, he’s been uploading videos of his finger dance routines for the public to see.

Months ago, Colina teased that he auditioned for America’s Got Talent season 18. He subsequently posted an official announcement that he was joining the internationally acclaimed talent show.

Since then, the finger dancer has been updating fans about his AGT journey. This was of course a nice treat to his supporters who wanted to know how he’s been gearing up for the competition.

If you want to see more of Colina on AGT, catch him on Tuesday on NBC at 8 p.m. EST.
