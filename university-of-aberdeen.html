University of Aberdeen | Scotland.org

Over the past 525 years, the University of Aberdeen has proudly offered its students life-changing opportunities by proving true to its foundational purpose, ‘open to all and dedicated to the pursuit of truth in the service of others’. The University’s vast experience in teaching – along with its modern 21st century outlook – enables it to offer the same great opportunity to you today.

Its rich heritage, world-leading research and learning excellence has contributed to associations with five Nobel Prize winners and, more recently, being named as a top 30 UK university by The Times and Sunday Times Good University Guide 2020. 

You are warmly invited to join the Aberdeen family and become part of the next chapter in its history.
