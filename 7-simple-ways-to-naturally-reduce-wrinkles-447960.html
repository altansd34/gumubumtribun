6. Rejuvenate your skin with a homemade apple and oatmeal mask.

You will need:

  • Oatmeal

  • Honey

  • Applesauce

  • Rosewater

How to prepare:

  • Put 2 tbsp applesauce and 1 tbsp honey into a mixing bowl.

  • Use a microwave to warm it up until the honey dissolves.

  • Add 2 tbsp oatmeal, and mix carefully.

  • Add 2 or 3 drops of rosewater, and mix again.

  • Let the mask cool.

How to use:

  • Apply the mask to a clean face and neck.

  • Wait for 15 minutes.

  • Use lukewarm water to wash your face. Immediately after that, rinse again with cold water.
