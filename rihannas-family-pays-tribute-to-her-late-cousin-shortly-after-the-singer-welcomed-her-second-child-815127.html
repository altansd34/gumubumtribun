Priyanka Chopra Shares Sweet Family Photos and Reveals Why She Used a Surrogate

Priyanka recently shared cute family photos with the main star of the show — their little daughter Malti Marie. However, there’s more to this story than meets the eye. Priyanka also opened up about a deeply personal choice — using a surrogate. She revealed the reason and the reaction of her fans.

Nick and Priyanka had a great summer vacation.

Priyanka has finally taken a break from her hectic shooting schedule. She’s been traveling all over Europe with her friends and family this summer. Besides, in a recent interview, Priyanka revealed she’s found inner peace in her daughter, “Now, I feel I have a center, a sense of calm because every decision ends up being about her.”

Priyanka posted a series of photos on Instagram.

The star shared some photos of Nick and her daughter Malti Marie Chopra Jonas. In one snap, Malti wears a cute white and pink floral onesie, with a matching headband, and even her doll has the same outfit. In another pic, Chopra’s lifts her little one up on her knee as both peer through a big glass door. Priyanka shared a lot more pics from a fun family day and the photos are charged with cuteness.

Fans were amazed.

Fans couldn’t help but sigh with adoration when they saw the photos. One user wrote, “The matching baby doll outfit and the jean jacket! Could not be any cuter than that.” Some were amazed by how fast their daughter grew, “Oh my goodness, is she walking already?” Another fan left a sweet comment, “Both of you are not only externally beautiful but also radiate beauty from within.”

The baby was born from a surrogate.

Priyanka had some health issues, so surrogacy became the best option. She recollects, “Our surrogate was so generous, kind, lovely, and funny. She took care of this precious gift for us for 6 months.” Some criticized her for the decision, and the star responded, “I’ve developed a tough hide when people talk about me. But it’s so painful when they talk about my daughter. I’m like, ’Keep her out of it.’”
