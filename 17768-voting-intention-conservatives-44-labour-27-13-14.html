Voting Intention: Conservatives 44%, Labour 27% (13-14 Mar)

Latest YouGov Westminster voting intention figures

This week's YouGov/Times voting intention figures see the Conservatives on 44% (the same as last week) and Labour on 27% (from 25% last week), giving the Tories a 17 point lead.

Elsewhere the Liberal Democrats remain on 10%, UKIP are on 9% (from 11% last week), and votes for other parties remain at 9% (from 10%).

Theresa May continues to be the favoured choice for best Prime Minister, with 48% of people preferring her to Jeremy Corbyn. The Labour leader is backed by 14% of voters, whilst 38% don't know.
