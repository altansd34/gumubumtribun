Evelyn Alexandra Lozada (born December 10, 1975) is an American television personality, model and spokesperson. She worked as a secretary to an entertainment attorney, and later became co-owner of Dulce, a shoe boutique located in Coral Gables, Florida. Lozada was also raised as a Roman Catholic.
