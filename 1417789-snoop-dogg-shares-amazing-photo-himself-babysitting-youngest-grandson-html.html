- Calvin Cordozar Broadus, known to many as Snoop Dogg, is a grandfather of six

- Away from the music, glitz and glamour of celebrity life, he is a wonderful family man

- This was evidenced by a recent photo of him babysitting his youngest grandson Chateau

American artist Snoop Dogg, whose real name is Calvin Cordozar Broadus is definitely enjoying premium family time as a proud granddad of six children.

He shared a photo of himself babysitting his youngest grandchild, Chateau Broadus, who was born Wednesday, March 10, 2021.

Chateau Broadus

Chateau is the second-born child of Snoop's son, Cordell Broadus, and his wife Phia Barragan.

In the photo, a greying Snoop is seen having quality grandpa-grandson time with the little one neatly tucked in his arms.
