A Cat Was Brought Into an Office to Get Rid of Mice, but Started to Bring in Money Instead

We love kitties and it’s not just for their soft, furry tummies. They are also excellent hunters who can help us avoid domestic pests and rodents. And it was this hunting skill that began a whole story, that ended with the creation of a charity. Okay, we know it’s hard to see the connection between these 2 facts, so we will tell you everything from the beginning.

We at Bright Side are in a hurry to share this story with you about a cat who has found a way to get money from strangers.

This lovely kitty was adopted by the owner of the marketing agency GuRuStu, in Tulsa. The thing was that they needed to keep mice away, and having a cat was a great solution to help solve this problem. This fluffy guy quickly joined the team and started to love sitting by the window and watching what was happening on the street.

And all this seemed normal, an ordinary cat watching people passing by. But suddenly, the team found money on the floor near the door. They couldn’t understand where it had come from. The situation repeated itself again and again, and they were at a loss for how this was possible.

However, later they came up with a theory. The company’s office is located in the entertainment district. And a lot of people walk past the office door. So, what if people used money to play with the cat?

They decided to test their hunch and tried to slide a bill into the slot in the door. And the result didn’t keep itself waiting. The cat quickly reacted and grabbed it. The theory turned out to be true. And this is how it works:

The cat with an unusual talent became really popular, and many people would come by to play with him. Because of this, more money appeared inside the door and the team decided to find a worthy cause they could donate it to. They decided to give this money to a charity called Tulsa Day Center for the Homeless, because there are lots of homeless people in the area and the kitty himself was also homeless once.

This adorable thief became known as the CASHnip Kitty and people started to send him money and support this sweet guy. He continues to help people in need and lives a joyful cat life. But he will never be an ordinary cat. This little fluffy has some big power and he changes lives for the better.
