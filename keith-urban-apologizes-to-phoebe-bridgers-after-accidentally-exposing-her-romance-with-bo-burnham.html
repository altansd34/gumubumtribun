Global superstar Keith Urban recently reacted to his viral TikTok video that accidentally captured Phoebe Bridgers and Bo Burnham kissing. Bridgers and Burnham have been under-the-radar since January.

Keith Urban Reacts to Unintentionally Revealing Phoebe Bridgers, Bo Burnham’s Relationship

Urban is now aware that he has to be careful of when he takes videos to upload on TikTok. Speaking to Entertainment Tonight during Day 2 of CMA Fest 2023, the country crooner reflected on the unintended moment.

“Yeah, oh, I found out,” he said. “Phoebe, I’m so sorry.”

Urban and his wife Nicole Kidman were present at the Philadelphia leg of Taylor Swift‘s Eras Tour last month. Filming their concert experience, the couple shared to TikTok a clip from the show where they were singing to Swift’s 1989 hit “Style.” Apparently, hawk-eyed fans immediately noticed a possible romantic pair in the background, catching Bridgers and Burnham sharing an intimate kiss behind Urvan and Kidman.

“It felt very awkward,” he said. “I felt bad. I was just digging on the concert! You never know what’s gonna happen.”

After being photographed in Los Angeles, California early this year, fans have been speculating that love is brewing between Bridgers and Burnham. The two, however, neither confirmed nor denied the assumptions. They also haven’t responded to Urban after his statement.

He Says Taylor Swift is Among The Greatest Songwriters

Regarding his experience during the concert, Urban stated that he had a “blast” having his wife around. According to the “We Were Us” singer, the Eras Tour was a “great” and “fun” time.

“I mean, seriously, you know, she’ll be among the greatest songwriters period, she really will be, when it’s all said and done,” he said.

Urban’s Las Vegas residency will be starting in no time. Subsequently, the singer teased what fans can expect from his string of shows.

“We’re looking to maybe slip in some songs we’ve never done before,” he said.

The singer also shared that they might perform “one or two” of his newest songs.
