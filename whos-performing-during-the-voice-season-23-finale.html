The Voice Season 23 comes to a close on Tuesday night, as a winner will finally be announced. The finale will feature several performances from celebrity guests. Let’s take a look at who will be performing during Tuesday night’s show.

Who’s Performing on The Voice Finale?

During the first half of the two-part finale on Monday night, guest performers Lewis Capaldi and Alex Newell took the stage. Tuesday night’s show promises even more famous names, who will be performing ahead of the winner announcement.

That includes former coach Adam Levine, who will be taking the stage with his band Maroon 5 to perform their single “Middle Ground.” Another former The Voice coach, CeeLo Green, will also be performing during Tuesday’s show.

Other performers include Diplo and Lily Rose, Toosii, and the U.S. Army Field Band. Tuesday’s episode will also feature performances from the coaches with their Top 5 artists, who performed Monday night for their last chance at America’s vote.

In addition, special guests Camila Cabello, Jennifer Hudson, Nick Jonas, John Legend, Adam Levine, Gwen Stefani, Usher, Pharrell Williams Alicia Keys, Miley Cyrus and Dolly Parton will appear on the episode to commemorate Blake Shelton’s last season.

Blake Shelton Is Bidding Farewell to the Show

Tuesday night’s finale also marks the show’s farewell to coach Blake Shelton. This will be Shelton’s final episode as a coach, after having appeared on every season since the show’s inception back in 2011. There are sure to be tributes to Shelton during the episode.

Shelton also has a chance to win his tenth season as a coach. He has two artists in the Top 5 — Grace West and NOIVAS. Meanwhile, each of his fellow coaches has one artist in the finale — Team Niall’s Gina Miles, Team Chance’s Sorelle, and Team Kelly’s D.Smooth.
