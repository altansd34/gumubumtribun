15+ Movie Couples Who Had Strong Chemistry Despite Their Big Age Difference

Harley Quinn and The Joker, Clementine and Joel, and Kate and Leo — these couples have become iconic. We worried about their relationships, sometimes more than our own, and believed in their love. In these duos, the actors and actresses were a perfect match, but we had no idea that they had a significant age difference between them.

At Bright Side, we want to recall our favorite big-screen couples in which one of the partners was significantly older than the other.

She’s 57, he’s 39.

He’s 69, she’s 30.
