Brooke Burke Joins The Cast of 'Penn &amp; Teller: Fool Us' Season 10

Magic is back with a brand new season of Penn & Teller: Fool Us. Now, there’s a new face in town to guide us through the mesmerizing world of illusions. Brooke Burke, renowned author and actress, will take the reins as the host for the show’s exciting tenth season.

Brooke Burke to Host Penn & Teller: Fool Us Season 10

For the past seven seasons, Alyson Hannigan has graced the show as the host. However, Hannigan is currently competing on Dancing With The Stars Season 32, where she’s partnered with Sasha Farber.

It seems like the show’s producers had to make a change, and they found the perfect replacement in Burke, who is no stranger to the world of hosting herself. Interestingly, Burke has experience hosting and winner DWTS.

For those who may not know, Penn & Teller: Fool Us is a popular one-hour competition series that celebrates the art of magic and features the legendary magician duo, Penn & Teller. The show’s premise is simple yet captivating. It invites aspiring magicians to perform their best tricks in an attempt to outwit the renowned Penn & Teller.

The catch is that the dynamic duo only gets to see the trick once and must immediately figure out the secrets behind it. If a magician manages to baffle Penn & Teller successfully, they then earn the coveted opportunity to perform with them in their celebrated show at the Rio Hotel & Casino in Las Vegas.

The show offers a real treat for magic lovers as the live studio audience and TV viewers watch alongside Penn & Teller with no camera tricks, secret edits, or helpful camera cuts. It’s all about the real magic.

Fans Have High Hopes For Brooke Burke as The New Host

The introduction of Burke as the new host is generating excitement among fans. Burke is a multi-talented television host, actress, and much more. She is also a cancer survivor, entrepreneur, fitness educator, philanthropist, and podcaster, making her a versatile addition to the show’s lineup.

Burke’s career spans numerous successful network television gigs and high-profile brand partnerships. Her impressive track record includes hosting the award-winning educational show Hidden Heroes and also co-hosting the iHeartRadio podcast Intimate Knowledge.

The tenth season of Penn & Teller: Fool Us is set to premiere on Friday, October 27, at 8:00 p.m. ET on The CW. This milestone season promises to deliver more mind-boggling magic, thrilling performances, and certainly plenty of surprises with the talented Burke leading the way.
