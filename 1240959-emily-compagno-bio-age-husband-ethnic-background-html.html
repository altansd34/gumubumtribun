Education

Fox News' Emily Compagno attended the University of Washington and graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Political Science. While she was still in the University, she was awarded the Air Force Reserve Officer Training Corps' Cadet of the Quarter Award.

She then joined the University of San Francisco School of Law and graduated with a Juris Doctor in 2006.

Legal career

Is Emily Compagno a practising attorney? Yes, she is. Shortly after her graduation, Emily began working in San Francisco as a criminal defence attorney. Next, she worked for John T.
