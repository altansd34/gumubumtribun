'America's Got Talent' Season 12 Will Premiere on May 30 With New Host!

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

Reading Time: 1 minutes

NBC’s popular show America’s Got Talent Season 12 is just around the corner. On May 30, the talent competition will be back on TV presented by new host Tyra Banks and judges Simon Cowell, Howie Mandel, Heidi Klum, and Mel B.

The talent variety show is the truest display of the best talent America has to offer, featuring a colorful array of singers, dancers, comedians, impressionists, magicians, contortionists, jugglers, ventriloquists, and hopeful stars all vying for their chance to win America’s hearts, (and of course), the $1 million prize!

The show has produced some big stars in the past, both from winners and finalists alike. The most recent winner, 12 year-old singer / songwriter Grace VanderWaal, joins a long list of champions who happen to be singers like Bianca Ryan (Season 1), Kevin Skinner (Season 4), Michael Grimm (Season 5), and Eugene Murphy Jr. (Season 6).
