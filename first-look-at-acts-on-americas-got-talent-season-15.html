‘America’s Got Talent’ returns this spring for Season 15 and we can’t wait. Whilst we patiently wait for an air date announcement, here are some sneak peek looks of acts we can expect to see! Buckle in, because this may just be the most entertaining season yet…

Noah Epps

Noah Epps is an 11-year-old dancer, model and actor. He also has a blackbelt in Taekwondo! Noah announced in an Instagram post (see above) that he is auditioning for America’s Got Talent this year. This may not be the first time you’ve heard of Noah, however. He has starred in the film, ‘Liam Payne & French Montana: First Time’ and took part in the World of Dance Championships. Judging by Noah’s past work in dance, he is a street dance act. And he’s pretty damn good! We can’t wait to see him on the AGT stage.

Watch Noah Epps in action below at the World of Dance Championships.

Divas of Compton & Drummers of Compton

Now, this is an act worth tuning in for! Combined with a group of drummers and dancers from the LA suburb of Compton, this act provides everything you need to be uplifted for the rest of your day! These guys haven’t been hiding in the shadows either. They have appeared on the TV show, ‘Bring It‘ where they have competed with other dance groups. The group haS also appeared on NBC access, The Timmi Mac Late Show and many more other big network shows! Combined with the Drummers of Compton, this fast-paced hip hop act already has our heart pumping!

Watch the Divas and Drummers of Compton in action below on the Tammi Mac Late Show!

Voices Of Our City Choir

This amazing choir based in California performs across the state, as a non-profit organiZation to help the growing crisis of homelessness. This choir is bound to bring a tear to all our eyes, singing everything from soul music to popular pop classics. This group is all about happiness and love, in the cause of raising awareness of the unsheltered. We’re rooting for you this year on AGT!

Watch the Voices Of Our City choir in action below in their latest promo reel!

Malik DOPE Drummer

With a social media presence of almost 100K, Malik already has a name for himself and he already comes to AGT with fans cheering him on. We are yet to find out if he will make it past the auditions.

Viral Tik Tok Sensation Zach Pincince Auditioned For ‘America’s Got Talent’

Although Zach did not make it past the auditions, we do know that the show reached out to him to audition which already means he has a name in the industry. Given how young he is, we can’t wait to see what he does. Fun fact, he has 3.2 fans on Tik Tok and has videos on there that constantly keep going viral.

Anthony Vito Rodriguez

Although this dancer has not confirmed if we will see him for sure, pretty sure the show has restrictions and even if he wanted to, he can’t say much. Here is hoping we see Anthony on the show.
